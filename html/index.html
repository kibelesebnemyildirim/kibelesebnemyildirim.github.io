Focus Areas

Research Interests

01

Healthcare Operations

Optimizing care delivery systems — from home care worker assignment to community pharmacy access — using operations research and data-driven methods.

02

Machine Learning & Optimization

Developing models that integrate simulation, stochastic optimization, and ML to support managerial decision-making under uncertainty in complex operational settings.

03

Public Systems & Crisis Management

Applying systematic operations research frameworks to wildfire management, rural infrastructure, and other high-stakes public system challenges.
