About Me

I am Kibele Sebnem Yildirim, a dedicated researcher in Operations Management and the founder of UniPath. With a strong background in Computer Science and Economics, I am passionate about applying operations research to solve real-world problems. My work includes projects on wildfire management, drone delivery in rural areas, and optimizing home care worker assignments.

UniPath

As the founder of UniPath, I am proud to introduce Turkey's first and only free virtual coach dedicated to guiding students who aspire to study abroad. UniPath is a unique peer-to-peer coaching platform that provides free counseling and support to students on their international education journey.

We offer a range of accessible resources, including:

  • Instagram live sessions with students from top colleges
  • YouTube videos on the college application process
  • Medium blog posts with valuable insights
  • Spotify podcasts featuring expert advice

Our innovative approach fosters peer-to-peer learning and has successfully engaged over 3,000 Instagram followers and 1,000 YouTube subscribers within a year. We are honored to have won first place in "Celebrating High School Innovators" and to have represented Turkey in the Global Youth Entrepreneurship Challenge 2021.
