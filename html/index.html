Hi there, I am Sebnem!

I am a fourth-year Computer Science and Economics student at University of British Columbia, focused on using computational methods to tackle complex real-world challenges. My work includes optimizing healthcare delivery, such as home care assignments and rural pharmacy access via drones, and developing an AR application for intravenous access. My projects leverage machine learning, data optimization, and simulation to deliver impactful, data-driven solutions. I am excited to connect with you and learn more about potential research that we might achieve together!
