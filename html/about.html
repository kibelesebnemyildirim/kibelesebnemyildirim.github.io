About Me

With a passion for using computational methods to address real-world challenges, my projects range from healthcare delivery innovations—like optimizing home care and expanding pharmacy access in rural areas with drones—to developing AR tools for medical use. With a focus on data-driven solutions, I’m eager to collaborate on projects that harness machine learning to drive impactful change in operations and management.
